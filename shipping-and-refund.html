Shipping and Refund Policy

Welcome to Yesareem Learning!

Yesareem is committed to delivering your order within the fastest time frame while ensuring the highest quality packaging. All orders are shipped within a week’s time and are mostly delivered within 4 – 5 working days from the date of dispatch. But if the Delivery address belongs to a remote location or in situations of strikes, natural disasters, inclement weather, governmental acts, or any other situation beyond our reasonable control, the delivery might be delayed. We ship throughout the week, except for Public holidays or in case of Govt. enforced Lockdown or any other situation which can prevent Delivery operations.To ensure that your order reaches you in good condition, in the shortest span of time, we ship through industry leaders in Logistics services.
